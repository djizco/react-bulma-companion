# Tag

### Small **tag labels** to insert anywhere

[Bulma Documentation](https://bulma.io/documentation/elements/tag/)

Supported Versions: `0.9.2`, `0.9.1`, `0.9.0`

## Default

The `<Tag>` component is a small but versatile element. It's very useful as a way to attach information to a block or other component. Its size makes it also easy to display in numbers, making it appropriate for long lists of items.

By default, a `<Tag>` is a `1.5rem` high label.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Default">
    <Tag>Tag Label</Tag>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Sizes

The `<Tag>` comes in 3 different sizes by use of the `size` prop.

While the default size is the **normal** one, the `size="normal"` prop exists in case you need to reset the tag to its normal size.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Sizes">
    <Tags>
      <Tag size="normal">Normal</Tag>
      <Tag size="medium">Medium</Tag>
      <Tag size="large">Large</Tag>
    </Tags>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

You can change the size of **all** tags at once with the `<Tags>` component.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Group Sizes">
    <React.Fragment>
      <Tags size="medium">
        <Tag>All</Tag>
        <Tag>Medium</Tag>
        <Tag>Size</Tag>
      </Tags>
      <Tags size="large">
        <Tag>All</Tag>
        <Tag>Large</Tag>
        <Tag>Size</Tag>
      </Tags>
    </React.Fragment>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

You can however keep the original size of a **subset** of tags, simply by applying one of its modifier props:

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Group Sizes with Subset">
    <Tags size="medium">
      <Tag>Medium</Tag>
      <Tag size="normal">Normal</Tag>
      <Tag>Medium</Tag>
      <Tag size="large">Large</Tag>
      <Tag>Medium</Tag>
    </Tags>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Modifiers

You can add the `rounded` prop to make a **rounded** tag.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Rounded">
    <Tag rounded>Rounded</Tag>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

You can add the `delete` prop to turn the tag into a **delete button**.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Delete">
    <Tag delete/>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Combinations

You can append a `<Delete>` component.

  <Story name="Combinations">

      </Tag>
