# Notification

### Bold **notification** blocks, to alert your users of something

[Bulma Documentation](https://bulma.io/documentation/elements/notification/)

Supported Versions: `0.9.2`, `0.9.1`, `0.9.0`

## Default

The `<Notification>` component is a simple colored block meant to draw the attention to the user about something. As such, it can be used as a pinned notification in the corner of the viewport. That's why it supports the use of the `<Delete>` component.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Default">
    <Notification>
      <Delete />
      Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit lorem ipsum dolor. <strong>Pellentesque risus mi</strong>, tempus quis placerat ut, porta nec nulla. Vestibulum rhoncus ac ex sit amet fringilla. Nullam gravida purus diam, et dictum <a>felis venenatis</a> efficitur. Sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit
    </Notification>
  </Story>
</Canvas>
